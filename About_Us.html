About US

This is a platform dedicated to promoting mental health awareness and support in Nepal. Mental health is an essential yet often overlooked part of well-being, and in Nepal, it faces significant challenges. With limited awareness, stigma, and a lack of accessible services, many individuals have difficulty accessing mental health services. The mental health need is immense in country like Nepal which is geographically complex, and has seen years of war durin Maoist insurgency, the big earthquakes, and the various natural disasters that frequently affect the lives of general people and add burden, not only financial but more significantly psychological. Most of our youths find the country with no scope for thier future ambitions and are compelled to migrate abroad. Many time the families who are left behind have to suffer the consequences of migration. Upto now, in Nepal there are only 241 registered psychiatrists and around 35 clinical psychologist to serve 30 million people. The prevalence of mental health issues is as high. According to the National Mental Health Survey conducted in 2019 and 2020 reports that 23% of the Nepali population are seeking treatment for mental health issues. The sucide rate is nearing 20 per day with 7000 people dying every year. The allocated budget for mental health is very minimal. The ratio of mental health professionals to general population is staggering In Nepal. Mental health in nepal is thus plaqued by dire shortage of human resouces, the priority of the government is still in physical infrastructure and other developmental tasks, stigma is prevalent in nepalese society and is a buring issue in rural communities where rituals such as "chhaupadi" is still practiced. well-being.

Through evidence-based information, expert insights, and personal stories, we aim to create a space where mental health is prioritized, understood, and accessible to all.

platform offers a wide range of resources, from educational content to professional guidance, all designed to foster a culture of compassion, understanding, and mental well-being. Whether you are a professional in the field or someone looking for support, our goal is to empower you with the tools and knowledge needed to navigate mental health challenges effectively.
